# 🇳🇴 Norsk Årshjul

### *Living the Norwegian Year*

A free, open-source calendar that brings together Norwegian holidays, official flag days, royal observances, Sámi celebrations, seasonal traditions, Norse heritage, and the natural rhythm of the year into one beautifully curated calendar.

Whether you have Norwegian ancestry, are learning the language, are planning to move to Norway, or simply love its culture, Norsk Årshjul is designed to help you experience the Norwegian year one day at a time.

---

# Why I Built This

I've always had a yearning to connect with a culture I couldn't access.

My ancestors came to the United States from Norway many years ago, but over the generations, the culture didn't survive. I grew up without the traditions that had once connected my family to our homeland. We celebrated familiar American holidays like the Fourth of July and Christmas, but there was very little warmth, tradition, or comfort in my home.

As I journey into motherhood, I find myself yearning once again for a sense of culture and tradition. Norwegian music, history, culture, and mythology have always awakened something in me. They remind me that life can be lived in rhythm with the land, the sky, and the changing seasons.

I want to give my daughter a foundation that's stronger than a handful of bank holidays each year. I want her to grow up looking forward to seasonal traditions, celebrating the return of the sun, noticing the first berries of summer, honoring the harvest, learning gratitude for nature, and understanding the stories that shaped the people who came before us.

I created **Norsk Årshjul** because I wanted those reminders for myself. A calendar that doesn't simply tell me what day it is, but invites me to participate—to cook traditional foods, celebrate Norwegian holidays, observe the changing seasons, learn the language, and reconnect with a heritage that my family lost over time.

If this project helps someone else rediscover a piece of their own heritage—or simply fall in love with Norway the way I have—then it has accomplished exactly what I hoped it would.

# Features

- 🏛️ Norwegian public holidays
- 🚩 Official Norwegian flag days
- 👑 Royal Family observances
- 🦌 Sámi cultural observances
- 🌲 Traditional Norwegian folk observances
- ⚔️ Norse / Ásatrú observances *(clearly identified when reconstructed)*
- 🌞 Solstices and equinoxes
- 🌿 Seasonal reminders inspired by Norway's natural year
- 📖 Norwegian vocabulary integrated into events
- 📱 Compatible with Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, Outlook, and any calendar supporting `.ics` subscriptions

# Project Philosophy

Norsk Årshjul is built around one simple idea:

> **Culture is experienced through rhythm.**

A calendar should do more than remind us of appointments. It should remind us to notice the changing seasons, celebrate meaningful traditions, and remain connected to the stories that shaped the people who came before us.

Historical accuracy matters.

Whenever traditions are modern reconstructions rather than historically documented observances, they will be clearly identified.
