# Project Vision

Norsk Årshjul exists to help people reconnect with Norwegian culture through the rhythm of the year.

The project combines Norwegian civic holidays, royal observances, traditional folk customs, Norse / Ásatrú observances, astronomy, and seasonal traditions into a single curated calendar experience.

The calendar is designed for people with Norwegian heritage, language learners, future immigrants, cultural enthusiasts, and anyone wishing to experience the Norwegian year more intentionally.
